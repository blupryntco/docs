**Compliance Hub** is Bluprynt's supervision layer. It gives regulators, compliance officers, and counterparties a single pane of glass for every asset under Bluprynt's umbrella — who issued it, what disclosures are on file, what credentials are active, and what's happening on-chain in real time.

## What you can see

- **Every attestation** written against the asset (KYI, MiCA, RegD, proof-of-collateral, custom).
- **Every disclosure filed** — with links to the IPFS artifact and the on-chain attestation hash.
- **Every credential active** — licenses, exemptions, jurisdiction-specific credentials.
- **The issuer record** — verified identity, KYB status, operating jurisdictions.
- **Policy module history** — which compliance rules are attached to the asset's token contracts.

## What actions you can take

Depending on your role:

- **Issue regulator notifications** — from pre-filled templates by jurisdiction + regulation.
- **Request amendments** — flag a filing that needs revision.
- **Export reports** — CSV, PDF, or structured JSON for audit trails.
- **Watch an asset** — receive notifications on new attestations, revocations, or policy changes.

## Access

Compliance Hub access is permissioned. To request access:

- **Regulators** — email [regulators@bluprynt.com](mailto:regulators@bluprynt.com).
- **Compliance officers at regulated institutions** — request access via your Bluprynt account manager.
- **Researchers and auditors** — read-only access may be granted on a case-by-case basis; contact [support@bluprynt.com](mailto:support@bluprynt.com).

<Warning>
  Compliance Hub exposes sensitive, jurisdiction-scoped data. Access requests are reviewed individually — there's no self-service signup.
</Warning>

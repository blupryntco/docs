## Who this is for

Anyone who will **author** disclosures, operate assets, or supervise issuers. Integrators who just need to embed the KYI widget don't need a full account — see [KYI Widget integration](/integration-guides/kyi-widget) instead.

## Sign up

<Steps>
  <Step title="Choose your signup path">
    - **Standard signup:** [app.bluprynt.com/signup](https://app.bluprynt.com/signup)
    - **MiCA-regulated issuer:** [app.bluprynt.com/signup-mica](https://app.bluprynt.com/signup-mica) — a streamlined flow tailored to EU Markets in Crypto-Assets Regulation requirements.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your corporate email">
    Use an email associated with the organization you'll file on behalf of — consumer emails (gmail, outlook, etc.) are blocked.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm your email">
    Click the link Bluprynt sends you. You'll land back on the app to complete onboarding.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete your profile">
    Provide your name, phone, address, and the company you represent.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Verify your organization

Before you can publish disclosures, your organization must complete **KYB (Know Your Business)** via Sumsub.

<Tip>
  KYB typically takes **under 24 hours** during business hours. You can continue with non-publishing work while verification is pending.
</Tip>

The flow is embedded directly in the Admin Console — follow the prompts. We ask for:
- Company registration documents
- Beneficial owner identity verification
- Proof of address / operating-jurisdiction documentation

## Pick a plan

Plans range from *Starter* (one asset, one active disclosure) to *Enterprise* (unlimited, plus dedicated support). Billing is handled through Stripe. See [bluprynt.com/pricing](https://bluprynt.com) for current tiers.

## Invite your team

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Settings → Members">
    Only organization owners and admins can invite.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add emails and pick roles">
    Roles: **Owner** (everything), **Admin** (everything except billing), **Member** (author + file), **Viewer** (read-only).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send invites">
    Invitees receive a tokenized link. Clicking the link + creating an account auto-links them to your organization.
  </Step>
</Steps>

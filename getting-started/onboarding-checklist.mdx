<Accordion title="Step 1: Creating your organization">
1. [Sign up](https://app.bluprynt.com/login) for a Bluprynt account.
2. To create off-chain disclosures only, go to **Smart Disclosures**.
3. To enable **onchain compliance when creating your disclosures in Smart Disclosures**, create an organization:
   1. Click your profile (bottom-left of the dashboard) → **Settings** → **Organization Settings**.

<Info>
  Note, in order to use Know Your Issuer (KYI), you must create an organization first, given that KYI is an organization-level attestation.
</Info>
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Step 2: Inviting your team">
1. Go to the home page of the dashboard
2. Navigate to the bottom lefthand corner of the dashboard
3. Click your profile (bottom-left of the dashboard) → **Settings** → **Organization Members**.
4. Enter the email and role of the invitee and press the "Invite" button.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Step 3: Using KYI">
<Info>
  **Note:** If you are (or represent) an asset issuer with an asset ready to issue, it's best to create an organization first and then go through the Know Your Issuer (KYI) process.
</Info>

1. Create an organization (go back to [#step-1-creating-your-organization](#step-1-creating-your-organization "mention"))
2. Navigate to the home page of the dashboard.
3. Click on "Authenticate Asset" button on the Know Your Issuer (KYI) card in the middle of the page.
4. Finalize the steps listed here: [how-it-works](/kyi-know-your-issuer/how-it-works "mention")
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Step 4: Using Smart Disclosures">
To use Smart Disclosures, please follow these instructions: [how-it-works](/smart-disclosures/how-it-works "mention")
</Accordion>

## Account Tiers & Credit Model

Bluprynt operates on a credit-based subscription model, designed for flexibility and scalability. Each credit grants the ability to publish one Smart Disclosures disclosure or generate an onchain attestation. This ensures costs scale with usage, not complexity.

The initial release of **Know Your Issuer (KYI)** is **free** for all qualifying assets, lowering the barrier to verifiable onchain identity.

Below are the current account tiers and included credit allocations.&#x20;

| Account Tier | Features | Annual Cost |
|---|---|---|
| Free | Free access to KYI | $0.00 |
| Starter | Access to Smart Disclosures &#x26; KYI; 2 credits + $2.5k per extra credit | $5,000.00 |
| Pro | Access to Smart Disclosures &#x26; KYI; 10 credits + $2k per extra credit | $10,000.00 |
| Enterprise | Inquiry Only | Inquiry Only |


## Permission levels

Accounts can create organizations for multiple users to collaborate on disclosures using Smart Disclosures. Below are the permission levels related to organization-based accounts:&#x20;

| Role | Capabilities |
|---|---|
| Administrator | Has all admin privileges |
| Editor | Can edit posts |
| Viewer | Can only view posts |
| Guest | Can only view posts they are inivted to |

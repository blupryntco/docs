The **Issuer Hub** is the workspace where token issuers run their compliance program end-to-end. It houses every product an issuer needs to verify their identity, file regulator-ready disclosures, demonstrate collateralization, and check their assets against jurisdictional requirements — all linked to a single verified onchain identity.

<Tip>
  If you operate a regulator or a third-party compliance team, you want the [Compliance Hub](/compliance-hub/compliance-hub-coming-soon) instead.
</Tip>

## Products in the Issuer Hub

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Know Your Issuer (KYI)" icon="id-card" href="/kyi-know-your-issuer/what-is-kyi">
    Cryptographically link your legal entity, controlling wallet, and token contract. Free for all qualifying assets and the foundation for every other product.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Smart Disclosures" icon="file-contract" href="/smart-disclosures/what-is-smart-disclosures">
    Author, validate, and publish regulator-ready disclosures. AI-assisted drafting with structured outputs in XBRL, PDF, and DOCX.
  </Card>
  <Card title="MiCA Checker" icon="circle-check" href="/mica-checker/mica-checker">
    Free diagnostic that scores a draft white paper against MiCA Articles 4 and 5 before you commit to a full filing.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Proof of Collateral" icon="vault" href="/issuer-hub/proof-of-collateral">
    Verifiable onchain attestation that a stablecoin or tokenized asset is backed by audited reserves of the disclosed type and amount.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## How the products fit together

<Steps>
  <Step title="Establish identity">
    Complete **KYI** to link your legal entity, controlling wallet, and token contract. Every other Issuer Hub product references this identity.
  </Step>
  <Step title="File your disclosures">
    Use **Smart Disclosures** to compose, validate, and publish regulator-ready filings. Run **MiCA Checker** first if you are launching into the EU.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Prove your reserves">
    Issue a **Proof of Collateral** credential to give exchanges, custodians, and counterparties a verifiable backing signal that travels with the asset.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Stay current">
    Renew expiring credentials, respond to supervisory requests from regulators in the [Compliance Hub](/compliance-hub/compliance-hub-coming-soon), and keep your asset queryable in the Bluprynt Registry.
  </Step>
</Steps>

**V4 Hooks** are Bluprynt's onchain integration layer for DeFi. They let any protocol read Bluprynt credentials — KYI, Proof of Collateral, and more — directly during a pool, swap, or lending operation, so tokenized assets only move through venues that match their compliance posture.

<Tip>
  V4 Hooks are how the Issuer Hub and Credentials pillars become enforceable on-chain. Without them, credentials are queryable; with them, credentials are *active*.
</Tip>

## Why V4 Hooks exist

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Stop contagion" icon="shield-virus">
    Block exposure to assets whose Proof of Collateral has expired or been revoked before contagion spreads through a lending market.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Gate liquidity" icon="filter">
    Restrict pool participation to KYI-verified assets, or to assets that satisfy a jurisdictional credential set.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Keep DeFi composable" icon="puzzle-piece">
    Compliance becomes a credential read, not a re-deployment. The same hook works across pools and protocols.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## How it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="A protocol attaches a Bluprynt hook">
    The pool or vault references a Bluprynt hook contract that knows how to query the Bluprynt Registry on its chain.
  </Step>
  <Step title="The hook reads credentials at operation time">
    On swap, deposit, borrow, or any other gated action, the hook fetches the credentials of the assets and parties involved.
  </Step>
  <Step title="The protocol enforces its rules">
    The hook returns the credential state. The protocol allows or rejects the operation based on the rules it published — for example, "only KYI-verified assets" or "Proof of Collateral active and not expiring within 7 days".
  </Step>
</Steps>

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="DEXs and AMMs" icon="arrow-right-arrow-left">

  </Card>
  <Card title="Lending and prime brokerage" icon="hand-holding-dollar">
    Refuse collateral whose Proof of Collateral has lapsed; reduce LTV when a credential is expiring.

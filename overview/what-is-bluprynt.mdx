Bluprynt is a **programmable compliance enforcement layer** for blockchain networks. It enables regulated digital assets to operate within existing legal frameworks while preserving the decentralization and transparency benefits of public ledgers.

## Why Bluprynt exists

The tokenized real-world-assets (RWA) market is projected to exceed **$16 trillion**, but institutional adoption has stalled because the underlying infrastructure cannot enforce regulatory requirements at the transaction level. Traditional Web3 systems can't check:

<Columns cols={2}>
  <Card title="Identity" icon="user-shield">
    Whether a wallet holder has met KYC / AML requirements in their jurisdiction.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Disclosures" icon="file-shield">
    Whether an asset has the regulator-ready disclosures that match its classification.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Licenses" icon="certificate">
    Whether the issuer holds the licenses required to operate in the markets they list in.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Policy" icon="gavel">
    Whether a given token operation (mint, transfer, burn) is compliant with jurisdiction-specific rules *at the time of transfer*.
  </Card>
</Columns>

Bluprynt fills each of those gaps with a dedicated product layer.

## What Bluprynt does

Four composable products form the **Compliance OS** for digital assets:

  <Card title="Identity" icon="id-card" href="/core-products/kyi/introduction">

  <Card title="Disclosures" icon="file-contract" href="/core-products/smart-disclosures/introduction">

  </Card>

## Bluprynt as an operating system

The four products compose cleanly because they sit on top of a shared platform:

- A **policy engine** that translates legal requirements into machine-readable modules. ([White paper](https://github.com/blupryntco/policy-engine))
- An **attestation service** that writes verifiable attestations across six blockchains. ([Repo](https://github.com/blupryntco/bluprynt-attestation))
- A **schema layer** that defines the shape of every identity, credential, and disclosure. ([Repo](https://github.com/blupryntco/schemas))
- A **public API** for programmatic integration. ([Repo](https://github.com/blupryntco/public-api))

## The four pillars

Bluprynt turns jurisdictional regulations, issuer disclosures, and regulator licenses into programmable enforcement logic for tokenized assets. Four products compose the platform.

<Columns cols={2}>
  <Card title="Know Your Issuer (KYI)" icon="id-card" href="/kyi-know-your-issuer/what-is-kyi">
    Cryptographic proof-of-issuance that prevents fraud in digital asset issuance by establishing verifiable links between issuers and their tokens.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Smart Disclosures" icon="file-contract" href="/smart-disclosures/what-is-smart-disclosures">
    Regulator-ready disclosure documents generated, validated, and published on-chain — with the authoring workflow baked in.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Compliance Hub" icon="shield-halved" href="/compliance-hub/compliance-hub-coming-soon">
    End-to-end supervision surface for regulators, compliance officers, and counterparties to review regulated asset activity in real time.
  </Card>
  <Card title="MiCA Checker" icon="circle-check" href="/mica-checker/mica-checker">
    Purpose-built compliance workflow for issuers subject to the EU Markets in Crypto-Assets Regulation.
  </Card>
</Columns>

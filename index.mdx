## The four pillars

Bluprynt turns jurisdictional regulations, issuer disclosures, and regulator licenses into verifiable onchain state. Four pillars compose the platform.

<Columns cols={2}>
  <Card title="Issuer Hub" icon="building-columns" href="/issuer-hub/overview">
    The issuer dashboard — Smart Disclosures, KYI, MiCA Checker, and Proof of Collateral in one workspace, bound to a single verified onchain identity.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Compliance Hub" icon="shield-halved" href="/compliance-hub/compliance-hub-coming-soon">
    The compliance dashboard for regulators and third-party compliance teams to review, decide, and monitor regulated asset activity in real time.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Credentials" icon="award" href="/credentials/credentials-overview">
    Verifiable onchain attestations of KYI and Proof of Collateral — portable, machine-checkable, and queryable across the asset lifecycle.
  </Card>
  <Card title="V4 Hooks" icon="plug" href="/v4-hooks/overview">
    Onchain integrations between Bluprynt credentials and DeFi protocols — keeping tokenized markets safe and free of contagion.
  </Card>
</Columns>

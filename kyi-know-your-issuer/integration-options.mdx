KYI attestations are designed to be easily integrated by platforms that need to verify token legitimacy and issuer identity.

We’re actively partnering with:

* **Block explorers**: to visually display which tokens are KYI-verified\\
* **Token listing services**: to prevent copycat or fraudulent submissions\\
* **Wallets and dApps**: to warn users or restrict interaction with unauthenticated tokens

Attestations can be queried via API or linked to token metadata, and are compatible with EVM, Solana, and ACE-enabled environments.
